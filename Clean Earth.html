The Pollution Crisis

Our planet is suffering from multiple types of pollution that threaten the future of life on Earth.

Air Pollution

9 out of 10 people breathe polluted air daily.

Water Pollution

80% of wastewater flows back untreated.

Plastic Waste

A garbage truck of plastic enters the ocean every minute.

E-Waste

50 million metric tons are generated annually.

Why It Matters Today

Pollution affects everyone. It contaminates our resources, causing health crises, harming wildlife, and accelerating climate change.

  • Air pollution causes millions of premature respiratory deaths.
  • Microplastics are now present in our entire food chain.
  • Contaminated water is destroying aquatic biodiversity globally.
